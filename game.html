Packing Suitcase Game

Can you pack the perfect suitcase for your trip in 60 seconds?

Game Instructions
info_outlineHow to Play
  1. Choose a destination from the dropdown menu
  2. You have 60 seconds to pack your suitcase with appropriate items
  3. Drag and drop items into your suitcase
  4. You need to think carefully about what items are appropriate for your destination!
  5. Essential items earn 20 points, useful items earn 10 points
  6. Unnecessary items will deduct 10 points and will be rejected from your suitcase
  7. Removing packed items will cost you points!
  8. You get bonus points for any time remaining on the clock
  9. Click "Ready to Go" when you're finished packing
